PHASE 1: CORE MISSION (Days 1-2)

Establish a clear, trustworthy brand message.

The Core Message

EN: "A gift only your child can design."

DE: "Ein Geschenk, das nur Ihr Kind gestalten kann."

The Trust Builder

Promise: 100% Money-Back Guarantee if the model doesn't resemble the drawing. We display this as a trust badge on the website.

Promise: 100 % Geld-zurück-Garantie, wenn das Modell der Zeichnung nicht ähnelt. Wir zeigen dies als Vertrauenssiegel auf der Website an.

The "Show & Tell" Kit

Prepare 10 kits, each containing a sample, the original drawing, and a display stand with a unique QR code for tracking.

PHASE 2: HYPER-LOCAL BLITZ (Days 3-10)

Generate buzz and a physical presence in Freiburg.

In-Person Placements

Objective: Secure placements for the "Show & Tell" Kits at key locations.

  • 🧸 Toy Store: Holzpferd Spielwaren (Holzstrasse 8)
  • 🎨 Art School: Jugendkunstschule Freiburg (Marienstraße 1)
  • 🏫 School: Lortzing-Grundschule (Lortzingstraße 1)
  • 👋 Youth Center: Jugendzentrum St. Georgen (Hartkirchweg 50)
  • ☕ Kids' Café: Café Krümel (Schiffstraße 9)

The Pitch Email (for Businesses)

Professional outreach to secure placements.

Subject: Eine einzigartige Geschenkidee für Ihre Kunden in Freiburg

Key Offer: "Für jeden Auftrag, der über Sie kommt, erhalten Sie 10€ Provision als Dankeschön."

PHASE 3: DIGITAL FOUNDATION (Days 11-20)

Build online presence and drive initial orders. Target: 2+ Orders.

"Exclusive Birthday" Social Campaign

CTA: "Order now for next month's birthday and get a €10 discount!"

Create "60-Second Case Study" Video

Show the drawing, the model, and the child's reaction. Feature this video on the website's landing page.

Engage with Local Parent Groups

DM 10+ Freiburg parent groups. Engage first, sell second.

Contingency Plan (If < 2 orders)

Launch a 48-hour "Freiburg Launch Special": 20% Discount + Free Shipping for the first 10 orders.

PHASE 4: PARTNERSHIPS & ENGAGEMENT (Days 21-30)

Scale reach through collaborations. Target: 7-8 Total Orders.

Partner with Event Planners

Contact 3 local planners with a win-win offer: 10% discount for their clients + €10 commission for them.

Outreach to "Mom Bloggers"

Offer a free, custom model in exchange for a story/mention. Personalize the outreach.

Host a Free Live Workshop

Venue ideas: Stadtbibliothek Freiburg, Jugendkunstschule. Live-model a drawing and offer an exclusive discount to attendees.

Launch "Drawing of the Month" Contest

Build an email list and gather user-generated content for your gallery. The winner gets a free Basic model.
